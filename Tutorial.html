How Does It Work

The homepage gives you two options, search by course or search by role.
Clicking on the given buttons will take you to the desired page.

The header provides you the option to go to the search pages directly.
Simply click on the Search option and select the option from the dropdown.

Once on the search page. Enter the course number in the "course" section.
Click on the search button on the bottom and the results will be displayed in the 'selected' box.
