Find Your Career in Cyber Security

Cyweb empowers users to take control of their cyber security career path. Cyweb uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) and ontologies to map Drexel's college courses to real work roles provided by the NICE framework.

Search by courses you're taking at Drexel to see what roles those courses are preparing you for. Or search by work roles you're interested in pursuing to see what courses at Drexel you can take to prepare you for those work roles.
